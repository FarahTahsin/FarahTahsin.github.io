I am a public health scientist with a PhD in Health Services Research and 8+ years of experience conducting population-level health research across primary care, mental health, and clinical services. My expertise spans mixed-method study design, epidemiological analysis, and translating evidence into actionable insights for health system improvement.

I have a proven track record managing multi-site research trials, analyzing administrative health data, and collaborating with interdisciplinary teams to address health equity and access challenges.

## Research Interests

- **Methods:** Qualitative and mixed-method research design, co-design methods, knowledge translation
- **Topics:** Patient-centered digital innovation, telehealth and treatment burden, health outcomes and evaluation, complex patients, multimorbidity
- **Skills:** Critical qualitative analysis, secondary administrative health data analysis, scoping reviews, survey development
